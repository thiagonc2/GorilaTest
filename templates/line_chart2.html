Calculator description:

This calculator evaluates the daily price of a single CDB investment given the date of the investment and the date of the evaluation. The CDB default value is of R$1000.00, the CDB price is calculated for each day between the initial and final date, the available dates for evaluation are between 2010-01-04 and 2019-12-03. Once the calculation was done, one can Download the "results.csv" file. Furthermore a log file, "cdbCalculator.log", is available for Download, with the history of the application.
